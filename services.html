ERASMUS PROJECTS

As an organization committed to promoting international mobility and collaboration, we actively participate in the Erasmus Plus program. Through this initiative, we facilitate opportunities for adult learners to engage in educational activities, such as job shadowing, staff training, and structured courses, across various European countries.

These programs enable participants to gain a deeper understanding of different cultures, expand their professional networks, and enhance their skill sets.

OUR COURSES

At European Teacher Courses, we offer a wide array of training programs tailored to adult learners' needs. Covering fields such as business management, entrepreneurship, digital marketing, IT, and language skills, our courses are designed to enhance existing skills or acquire new ones.

Being part of the Erasmus Plus program allows us to provide international mobility opportunities, including study visits, job shadowing, and training courses across Europe, enriching participants' learning experiences.
